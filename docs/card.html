Card

A card is a flexible and extensible content container. It includes options for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contextual background colors, and powerful display options.

Cards can have a wide variety of content, including images, text, list groups, different buttons and more.

Horizontal Card

Castor-UI provides two types of horizontal card. Card with image in center and card with image at the top of the card. You need to add card-horizontal class to make horizontal card and img-top class to make the image appear at the top, by default it is in center.
