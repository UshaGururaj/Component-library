Badge

Badges are small status descriptors for UI elements. Badge is used to add additional information to the content such as notification numbers or number of items in the cart or user status like online, offline, away.

Badge on Avatar

Badge on Avatar can show different user status, such as offline, online or away.

Badge status includes online badge, offline badge, away badge and number badge. You can add this badges at four positions, top left, top right, bottom right, and bottom left and also you can add different size badges. You have to add respective contextual classes such as badge-online , badge-offline , badge-busy , badge-num , top-left , top-right , bottom-right , bottom-left badge-l , badge-m , badge-s , badge-xm .
